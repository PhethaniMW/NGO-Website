About Us

Thusa Lushaka Empowerment is dedicated to empowering the youth through various initiatives, including educational support, pageants, and awards for excellence. Our mission is to provide opportunities and resources for young people to thrive and succeed.

Our Projects

Explore our initiatives:

  • Donations: Help us make a difference by contributing to our cause. Your donations go directly towards supporting our youth programs.
  • Pageants: Our annual youth pageants celebrate talent, intelligence, and beauty, providing a platform for young people to shine.
  • School Awards: Recognizing academic excellence and achievements in local schools, motivating students to strive for their best.
